All purchases made through Data Breach are final and non-refundable. We do not offer refunds under any circumstances.

We do not offer service credits or account balances for future purchases. All credits must be used within the purchased period.

While you may cancel your subscription at any time, no refunds will be provided for any remaining subscription period. Access will continue until the end of the current billing cycle.

If you believe a feature is not functioning as described, please contact our support team before requesting any dispute. We are committed to resolving technical issues, but this does not constitute grounds for a refund.

5. Chargebacks

Initiating a chargeback or dispute with your payment provider may result in immediate account suspension and termination of access to all services.

6. Before Purchasing

We strongly recommend:

7. Support Contact

If you have questions about this policy or need assistance, please contact our support team before making any purchase decision.
